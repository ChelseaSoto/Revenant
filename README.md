REVENANT: THROUGH THE VEIL README


Walkthrough:
Enter the Code Prototype level. Press Q to use the light ability. Reflect it off the blue box or panel to hit the ball and complete the puzzle. Press E to interact with the statue in the center of the room. The door in front of you will lead you to a sequence puzzle. Interact with the spheres in the right order (left to right) in order to unlock the door. Interact with the statue again and go to the right door. Follow the NPC closely as it guides you, then go through the door to come back to the first level.
Enter the Art Prototype level. You will see a chamber room and enemy type.


Features:
1. Main Menu
2. Combat
3. Light ability + reflections
4. Sequence Puzzle
5. NPC Companion
6. Enemy
7. Chamber Room


Controls:
Keyboard and Mouse:
1. W, A, S, D for directional movement
2. Space bar for jump
3. Hold Shift for sprinting, press for dodge
4. E to interact
5. Q to use ability
6. Mouse for camera control
7. Ctrl for lock-on
8. LMB for light/heavy attack
9. RMB for block/parry
Controller:
1. LS for walking and sprinting
2. A to jump
3. B for dodge
4. X for interact
5. LT to aim ability
6. RT to use ability
7. RS for lock-on and camera control
8. RB for light/heavy attack
9. LB to block/parry
